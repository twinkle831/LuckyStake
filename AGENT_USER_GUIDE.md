# Set-and-Forget AI Agent - User Guide

## What is Set-and-Forget?

The AI Agent's "Set-and-Forget" feature lets you deposit money **once** and let an intelligent system automatically distribute it across lottery pools based on your preferences. No more manual deposits every week—just set your strategy and the AI takes care of the rest.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Open the AI Agent

1. Connect your Stellar wallet to LuckyStake
2. Look for the **AI Agent** button (chat icon) in the navbar or bottom navigation
3. Click to open the AI Agent chat panel

### Step 2: Create a Strategy

1. In the AI Agent chat, click the **"🚀 Create Set-and-Forget Strategy"** button
2. A 3-step modal will appear

## Creating Your Strategy

### Step 1: Amount

**"How much do you want to deposit?"**

- Enter the total amount in XLM you want to invest
- Example: 100 XLM
- This is the total that will be distributed across pools
- Minimum: 0.0000001 XLM

### Step 2: Preferences

Choose your preferences:

#### Duration
- **1 week**: Fast turnover, frequent deposits
- **2 weeks**: Balanced approach
- **3 weeks**: Longer holding period
- **4 weeks**: Extended strategy

#### Risk Level
- **Low** (Conservative)
  - Focus on weekly draws
  - More frequent deposit opportunities
  - Lower variance in prize amounts
  - Best for: Risk-averse investors
  
- **Medium** (Balanced)
  - Mix across all pool types
  - Good balance of frequency and prize size
  - Moderate variance
  - Best for: Most investors
  
- **High** (Aggressive)
  - Focus on monthly draws
  - Less frequent but larger prizes
  - Higher variance
  - Best for: Risk-tolerant investors seeking big wins

#### Goal
- **Sure-Shot** (More chances to win)
  - Multiple smaller deposits
  - More frequent draw participation
  - Best for: Consistent winners
  
- **Highest-Prize** (Maximize prize pool)
  - Focus on high-value draws
  - Aim for biggest prizes
  - Best for: Jackpot seekers

### Step 3: Review Allocation

**"AI-Recommended Allocation"**

The AI will show you the recommended pool allocation:
- **Weekly Pool**: X% of your deposits
- **Biweekly Pool**: X% of your deposits  
- **Monthly Pool**: X% of your deposits

Example allocations:
- Low risk: 60% weekly, 30% biweekly, 10% monthly
- Medium risk: 40% weekly, 40% biweekly, 20% monthly
- High risk: 20% weekly, 30% biweekly, 50% monthly

**Review and confirm** - Once you're happy with the allocation, click **"Create Strategy"**

## What Happens Next

### Immediate (Strategy Created)
✅ Your strategy is now **active**  
✅ Automatic deposits will begin within 1 hour  
✅ Your dashboard shows the strategy card  

### Ongoing (Every 6 Hours)
✅ The system checks if it's time to make a deposit  
✅ Calculates the proportional amount based on your allocation  
✅ Deposits to the next assigned pool  
✅ Updates your execution history  
✅ Continues until balance is depleted or strategy completes  

### Monitoring Your Strategy

#### Dashboard Card
On your **Dashboard** tab, you'll see a card for each active strategy:

- **Status Badge**: Shows if it's "Active", "Paused", "Completed", or "Withdrawn"
- **Progress Bar**: Visual representation of how much has been deposited
- **Stats Grid**: 
  - Remaining balance (XLM left to deposit)
  - Executions completed (number of automatic deposits)
  - Next execution time (countdown to next deposit)
- **Pool Allocation**: Expandable section showing the distribution
- **Recent Deposits**: Last 3 deposits made

#### Execution History
Scroll down in the strategy card to see:
- Date of each deposit
- Which pool it went to
- Amount deposited
- Status (complete, pending, failed)

## Managing Your Strategy

### Pause Strategy
**When to use**: If you need to temporarily stop deposits

1. Click the menu (⋮) in the top-right of the strategy card
2. Select "Pause"
3. Automatic deposits will stop immediately
4. Your remaining balance stays in the strategy

**Resume**: Click menu → "Resume" to continue

### Withdraw Strategy
**When to use**: To get your money back and stop all deposits

1. Click the menu (⋮) in the strategy card
2. Select "Withdraw"
3. Confirm the withdrawal
4. Your remaining balance is returned to your wallet
5. Strategy moves to "Withdrawn" status

### Manually Trigger Execution
(Available in future updates)

If you don't want to wait for the automatic execution, you can manually trigger a deposit.

## How Deposits Work

### Automatic Distribution

Your total amount is divided based on the allocation:

**Example: 100 XLM, Low Risk**
- Allocation: 60% weekly, 30% biweekly, 10% monthly
- First cycle:
  - 1st deposit: 60 XLM → Weekly Pool
  - 2nd deposit: 30 XLM → Biweekly Pool
  - 3rd deposit: 10 XLM → Monthly Pool
- Total cycle repeats if balance remains

### Timing

Deposits happen every 6 hours (4 times per day):
- 12:00 AM (midnight)
- 6:00 AM
- 12:00 PM (noon)
- 6:00 PM

Each deposit cycles through your allocated pools in order.

### Your Tickets

When we deposit to a pool, you automatically get:
- Tickets for the deposit amount
- Entry in the next draw for that pool
- Chance to win the pool's prize

Example: If we deposit 60 XLM to weekly pool, you get approximately 6,000 tickets.

## Earnings & Withdrawals

### How Earnings Work

1. **Deposits accumulate tickets** in each pool
2. **Draws happen at scheduled times** (weekly on Monday, biweekly on the 6th, monthly on the 1st)
3. **If you win**, your winnings appear in your account
4. **You can withdraw anytime** - including your original principal

### Claiming Your Winnings

1. Go to **Dashboard** tab
2. Scroll to "Your Deposits"
3. Look for deposits with status "Claimable"
4. Click "Claim" to withdraw the amount

## FAQ

### Q: Can I change my strategy after creating it?
**A:** Yes! You can pause, modify preferences, or withdraw at any time. In future updates, you'll be able to update the allocation mid-strategy.

### Q: What if a deposit fails?
**A:** If a deposit fails (e.g., insufficient balance), your strategy automatically pauses and alerts you. You can resume once the issue is fixed.

### Q: How do I know my deposits are happening?
**A:** Check the strategy card on your dashboard:
- Progress bar shows total deposited
- Execution count shows number of deposits
- Recent deposits section shows the last 3

### Q: Can I withdraw before the strategy completes?
**A:** Yes! Click "Withdraw" anytime to get your remaining balance back.

### Q: What happens if I disconnect my wallet?
**A:** Your strategy continues executing automatically. Reconnect anytime to see updated status.

### Q: How much does this cost?
**A:** Using the set-and-forget feature is free! You only pay normal pool participation fees (if any).

### Q: Is my money safe?
**A:** Your funds are deposited to legitimate lottery pools on the Stellar network. All deposits are on-chain and verifiable.

### Q: What if I want to use a different strategy?
**A:** You can create multiple strategies! Each has its own allocation and schedule.

## Troubleshooting

### My strategy shows "Paused" but I didn't pause it
**Cause**: A deposit failed (likely insufficient balance)  
**Fix**: Add funds to your wallet and click "Resume"

### No deposits are showing up
**Cause**: Executor hasn't run yet (max 6 hours)  
**Fix**: Wait up to 6 hours, or check executionTime in strategy card

### AI recommendation failed
**Cause**: Network error or API issue  
**Fix**: Don't worry! System uses safe default allocations automatically

### I can't create a strategy
**Issue**: Wallet not connected  
**Fix**: Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
